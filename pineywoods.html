Piney Woods

MOST POSSIBLE Disaster: Forest Fire because it is literally full of trees. Its climate makes it the perfect place for a forest fire because it is hot, dry, and etc.

Some Major Cities In This Ecoregion Are: Nacogdoches, Marshall, Jacksonville

ESCAPE PLAN - HEAD NORTH TOWARDS OKLAHOMA TO SAFETY. IF THE PATH IS BLOCKED, PROCEED TOWARDS THE SOUTH OR HOLE UP IN A BUNKER(JK)! IF NOTHING ELSE WORKS, USE A SATELLITE PHONE TO CALL 911 AND HAVE THE CALLER SEND A HELICOPTER TO BE AIRLIFTED OUT OF THE REGION TO SAFETY.
