Gulf Coastal Plains

MOST POSSIBLE Disaster: Hurricane because the ecoregion borders the coastline and hurricanes constantly strike here.

Some Major Cities In This Ecoregion Are: Houston, Galveston, Corpus Christi

ESCAPE PLAN - IN THE CASE OF PRIOR WARNING TO HURRICANE, RUN. GET OUT OF THERE. IN THE EVENT YOU ARE CAUGHT IN A HURRICANE, HUNKER DOWN IN A TALL BUILDING(IF NEAR A COAST) OR FIND A SAFE PLACE THAT IS STURDY TO HIDE IN.
